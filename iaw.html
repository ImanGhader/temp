Islam Awareness Week

Art direction • Campaign design

IAW is an annual event run by the Muslim students' association, the largest association at Carleton Univerisy. I was approached by the coordinators of IAW to be the creative director and graphic designer of the marketing campaign for this event. It was run by 872 volunteers that handled an audience of approximately 536,536 people yearly. Being the creative director, I gathered and directed a marketing team over a 4 month preparation period. Directing included designing a marketing strategy to be executed before, during and after the event. Some examples of the final deliverables are shown bellow.
