MixMe App Design

UX Research • App Design

MixMe’s concept reverses the act of searching for the next meal to cook. Instead of wasting time looking for all types of recipes first, MixMe allows you to enter your already available ingredients and then suggests recipes based on what you have in your kitchen.

One of the apps main features is that it allows you to scan barcodes. The user can scan the barcode of individual items to enter them or scan the barcode of their receipt from the grocery store to add a list of groceries all together. That way the user can view other recipes than what they had in mind to do with their grocery items.
