Bashai Package Design

Package Design • Research

Bashai is a specialty tea package designed to serve guests in a formal manner. The concept was derived from the Moroccan culture, supporting the hospitality tradition of serving guests tea at all times of the day. The package was designed with accordance to the traditional cups used within the culture, in keeping the same shape and interaction. It’s prestigious design avoids the informality of presenting guests unwrapped tea bags. Hotels, formal parties and dinners are examples of where Bashai would be best served. It is therefore not a product sold on the shelves.

The fictional brand created perfectly combines two Arabic words, Basha and Shai. Basha, meaning Pasha or Master, stands representing the high-end audience this product is targeted for. It portrays elegance and generates a personal connection with the consumer. Shai on the other hand is simply representing the name of tea in Arabic.
