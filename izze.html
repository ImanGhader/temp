Izze Juice Campaign Design

Illustration • Campaign design

Izze is a sparkling drink company that was founded by two gentlemen in the USA. It’s marketing focuses on the usage of realistic images and very bright colour pallets, adhering to the vibrant colours of the product. During a college project, I created a new campaign for the company that presented a new advertising approach.
