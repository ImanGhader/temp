Adventure begins here.

I’m a designer based in Ottawa and I love watching choppy black and white musicals from the 60’s.

I’m not a problem solver, I’m an adventurer. Creating is what I do to adventure, to seek more knowledge, and in return live with meaning.

I call myself a creator because design is only one aspect of creating that allows me to tell the stories I explore. Adventure to me is anything and everything, including your next project.

That project is not a problem and I’m not the solution. It’s more of an adventure and together we're something like the story heroes.

If you wish to colaborate or simply talk over coffee drop me a line here.

How I do it.

Jim Jarmusch’s attitude about art sums up my design philosophy: creating a single good thing requires devouring everything. Out of the different types of work in my portfolio, the ones I enjoyed working on the most required expanding the delivery of a concept through different elements. I thrive in researching and exploring ideas, and I believe that puts me in a good place to approach and handle any type of project.
